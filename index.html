How To Play

(i) TENIZ is a spin-off on WORDLE, to test your knowledge on Tennis Grand Slam Champions of the Open Era. The objective is to identify the Tennis Player every day.

(ii) Use the on-screen keyboard to key in your answer. Hit Enter to submit. A new clue will be revelaed after each incorrect guess.

(iii) Hints include Year, Slam, Nationality, Gender, First Name First Letter and Last Name First Letter.

(iv) Pay attention to the number of letters in the players first and last names. This is another clue, that could be of help.

(v) A Green Tile highlight indicates that the right letter is at the right position, while a Yellow Tile highlight indicates that the right letter is at the wrong position. First and Last name are evaluated separately.

(vi) An Orange Key highlight indicates a letter part of the name yet to be correctly placed . Green Letter Tracker helps keep track of correctly placed letters.
