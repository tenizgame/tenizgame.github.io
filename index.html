How To Play

(i) TENIZ a game inspired by WORDLE, tests your knowledge on Tennis Grand Slam Champions of the Open Era. The objective is to identify the Champion Tennis Player every day, using the Least Number Of Clues.

(ii) Use the search area to key in your answer and Enter. A new clue will be revelaed after each INCORRECT guess.

(iii) Hints include Year, Slam, Country, Gender, Total GS Singles Titles and Style of Play (RH vs LH). Clues are revealed as shown below after each guess.

(iv) After each wrong guess, you get secondary clues, to show how the player you entered matched against the original clues (evaluated in order of original clues even if they are not yet revealed).
